# Introducing Castrel

We're excited to introduce **Castrel**, an AI-powered SRE Agent designed to transform how teams handle incidents, alerts, and operational tasks.

## The Problem

Modern infrastructure is complex. SRE teams are often overwhelmed by:

- **Alert fatigue**: Hundreds of alerts daily, many false positives
- **Incident response**: Manual investigation and diagnosis
- **Repetitive tasks**: The same runbooks executed over and over

## How Castrel Helps

Castrel acts as your always-on SRE teammate:

### 1. Intelligent Alert Triage

Castrel automatically analyzes incoming alerts, correlates them with historical data, and prioritizes based on actual impact.

### 2. Automated Investigation

When an incident occurs, Castrel immediately begins investigation:

- Queries relevant metrics and logs
- Identifies potential root causes
- Suggests remediation steps

### 3. Operational Automation

Common operational tasks can be delegated to Castrel, freeing your team to focus on strategic work.
